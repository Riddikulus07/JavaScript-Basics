Probability Statistics and Random Processes




This book with the right blend of theory and applications is designed to provide a thorough knowledge on the basic concepts of Probability, Statistics and Random Variables offered to the undergraduate students of engineering. Addition of important topics as per the syllabi requirements is the basis of this revision. This is the third edition of the bestselling title Probability, Statistics And Random Processes designed for undergraduate students. It provides a thorough introduction to the fundamental concepts of probability theory and random processes. Students of mathematics who have to study Probability and Statistics methods will find this book highly beneficial. It teaches students how these methods are executed.
